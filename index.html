About me

I'm Craig Adam, a Software Developer living in Shropshire.

I started my career in ICT in 2012, joining the Royal Air Force as a Cyber Space Communications Specialist. I spent the next 6 years working in Above Secret Information Systems Flight, where I led teams building, installing and supporting the UK's Top Secret ICT system, both in the UK and overseas.

In 2018 I became a Software Developer with the Learning Technologies Team at RAF Cosford. The team build bespoke web-based applications and media to be utilised by instructors and trainees at the Defence School of Aeronautical Engineering. I've built and project managed web apps focusing heavily on JavaScript, a programming language i'm well versed in and particularly enjoy. I've also been fortunate to learn C# and the Unity games engine, with the team focusing on the 'gamification' of training.
